Spectra 2.0

technical event hackathon workshop webinar

Spectra is the technical association day of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, GEC Palakkad. Every year we celebrate our association day with the aim of providing a platform for the Computer Science Student Community to develop and showcase their technical prowess

The technical events consist of several workshops, webinars, competitive coding, debugging and the ever popular gaming event. Spectra is the ideal celebration for all technophiles: young or old, beginner or expert, students to witness, learn and experience the wonders of computer science and technology. Boasting of a huge roster of exciting and engaging events, this edition of Spectra is going online promising to be grander, greater and more glorious than ever before.
